At Pet Health Aid, we focus on complete treatment of your pet, not just the disease symptoms. Our integrative approach blends traditional veterinary medicine with complementary therapies, and offers a balanced progress to your pet’s health and well-being. With this approach, we strive encourage natural healing and manage optimal health throughout your pet’s life.

Acupuncture

What is Acupuncture?

Acupuncture is a traditional Chinese Medicine practice that involves insertion of thin needles into specific points on the body to stimulate healing, reduce pain, and enhance overall health and balance.

Benefits of Acupuncture:

  • Pain relief
  • Improved circulation
  • Reduced inflammation
  • Enhanced immune system function
  • Treatment for chronic conditions such as arthritis, hip dysplasia, and neurological disorders

How it Works: In an acupuncture session, our skilled veterinarians carefully place sterile, thin needles into specific points on your pet’s body. Sessions usually last for 20 to 40 minutes, and offer a serene experience for most pets.

Chiropractic Care

What is Chiropractic Care?

Chiropractic care for pets is a practice that involves a manual manipulation of the spine and joints of the pet’s body to alleviate pain, improve mobility, and enhance overall well-being by restoring proper alignment and function.

Benefits of Chiropractic Care:

  • Improved mobility and flexibility
  • Relief from pain and discomfort
  • Enhanced nervous system function
  • Better posture and alignment
  • Support for recovery from injuries

How it Works: Veterinary experts at Pet Health Aid provide gentle adjustments to your pet’s spine and joints, correcting misalignments, if any, to restore their normal mobility and function. These adjustments prevent discomfort and improve overall well-being of your pet.

Herbal Medicine

What is Herbal Medicine?

Herbal medicine for pets involves use of natural plant-based remedies to support health and respond to various ailments. These treatments often harness the therapeutic properties of herbs to promote well-being and aid in healing.

Benefits of Herbal Medicine:

  • Natural support for the immune system
  • Reduction of inflammation and pain
  • Treatment for skin conditions, digestive issues, and respiratory problems
  • Gentle and holistic approach to managing chronic diseases

How it Works: Our experienced veterinarians suggest personalized herbal formulations After evaluating your pet's condition. These herbal remedies, whether used independently or aligning with other remedies, are designed to optimize effectiveness and improve your pet's well-being.

Nutritional Counseling

What is Nutritional Counseling?

Proper nutrition is a cornerstone of holistic health. Our nutritional counseling services provide personalized diet plans that cater to your pet’s specific needs, whether they require weight management, allergy support, or dietary adjustments due to health conditions.

Benefits of Nutritional Counseling:

  • Improved overall health and vitality
  • Support for the immune system
  • Management of chronic conditions such as diabetes and kidney disease
  • Optimal weight maintenance

How it Works: Our veterinarians will evaluate your pet’s current diet and health status, then create a customized nutrition plan. We’ll provide guidance on the best foods and supplements to support your pet’s health goals.

Homeopathy

What is Homeopathy?

Homeopathy is a holistic healing system that uses highly diluted natural substances to stimulate the body’s self-healing processes.

Benefits of Homeopathy:

  • Safe and gentle treatment option
  • Suitable for a wide range of conditions
  • Can be used alongside conventional treatments
  • Focus on treating the underlying cause of illness

How it Works: Our veterinarians will conduct a thorough assessment of your pet’s health and behavior to select the appropriate homeopathic remedies. These remedies are tailored to your pet’s individual needs and are designed to support their body’s natural healing abilities.

Why Choose Holistic Veterinary Care?

Comprehensive Approach: We treat the entire animal, considering physical, emotional, and environmental factors that may affect your pet’s health.

Natural Healing: Our holistic therapies support the body’s innate healing processes, providing a natural and gentle alternative to conventional treatments.

Individualized Care: We tailor our treatments to meet the specific needs of each pet, ensuring a personalized approach to their health and wellness.

Preventive Focus: Holistic care emphasizes prevention and early intervention, helping to maintain health and prevent disease before it starts.
